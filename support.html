Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to any server, synced to the cloud, or shared with anyone. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

How do I get started?

Open the app and follow the Setup Progress checklist on the Get Started tab. Enter your date of birth, add your retirement accounts, enter your income sources, and add any deductions. The app will calculate everything else automatically.

What tax year does the app use?

RetireSmart IRA uses 2026 federal tax brackets and state tax rates, including changes from the OBBBA (One Big Beautiful Bill Act).

How do I model a Roth conversion?

Go to the Scenarios tab and use the Roth Conversion slider to set the amount. The tax impact updates in real time on the right side of the screen, showing you the additional tax, effective rate, and Medicare IRMAA impact.

What are RMDs?

Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs) are mandatory annual withdrawals from Traditional IRAs and 401(k)s. Under SECURE 2.0, RMDs begin at age 73 (born 1951-1959) or age 75 (born 1960 or later). The RMD Calculator tab shows your current and projected RMDs.

Can I share my tax plan with my CPA?

Yes. Go to the Tax Summary tab and tap "Export PDF for CPA" to generate a detailed summary you can share with your financial advisor.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to view, modify, or cancel your subscription.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. RetireSmart IRA works entirely offline. The only network connection used is for subscription verification through Apple's App Store.
